My Discord Bot

A fun interactive companion for your Discord server!

Commands

  • !quiz

    Play a quiz game with a randomly selected question. Answer correctly to win!

    In this game, you'll be presented with a question and four possible answers. Choose the correct answer to earn points! The bot will keep track of your score and let you know how you're doing.

  • !guess

    Play a number guessing game. Try to guess a number between 1 and 100!

    In this game, you'll have to guess a number between 1 and 100. After each guess, the bot will give you a hint, such as "too high" or "too low", to help you narrow down the correct answer.

  • !hangman

    Play a game of hangman. Guess a letter to try to reveal the word!

    In this game, you'll have to guess a letter to try to reveal a hidden word. Each incorrect guess will bring the hangman one step closer to being complete! Guess the word correctly before the hangman is complete to win.

  • !rps

    Play a game of rock, paper, scissors against the bot. Type 'Rock', 'Paper', or 'Scissors' to make your choice!

    In this game, you'll play a best-of-three series against the bot. Choose your move wisely, as the bot will try to outsmart you!
